# Jaipur Ultra

## About the Brand

**Jaipur Ultra** is Jaipur's first and only ultra marathon event. A premier endurance sports experience that showcases the spirit of perseverance while celebrating the rich cultural and heritage tapestry of Rajasthan.

## Event Overview

Held annually in **November**, Jaipur Ultra starts from the iconic **Jal Mahal**, one of Jaipur's most recognizable landmarks. The event seamlessly blends extreme endurance sport with authentic Rajasthani culture and heritage, creating a unique racing experience.

## Race Categories

### 🏃 50 KM Ultra
The ultimate test of endurance for experienced ultra runners. Push your limits across 50 kilometers through the scenic landscapes surrounding Jaipur.

### 🚶 25 KM Challenge
A challenging but accessible distance for those seeking an endurance experience. Perfect for runners looking to take their fitness to the next level.

## What Makes Jaipur Ultra Unique

- **Heritage Starting Point**: Begin your journey from the majestic Jal Mahal
- **Cultural Integration**: Experience Rajasthani hospitality and culture throughout the event
- **Scenic Routes**: Run through Jaipur's beautiful and historic landscapes
- **Community-Driven**: A celebration of endurance sports and local pride
- **Inaugural Event**: Jaipur's first-ever ultra marathon experience
